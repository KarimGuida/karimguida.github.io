About

Hi! I'm Karim Guida, a Mathematician and Data Scientist with a PhD in Applied Mathematics and a strong academic background in teaching mathematics at the university level.

My journey into data science began with a passion for problem-solving and a deep curiosity about how data shapes the world around us.

I spent several years teaching mathematics, developing strong analytical thinking and communication skills. I also published over a dozen research papers in international journals, reflecting my dedication to rigorous analysis and real-world impact.

Driven by a desire to expand my expertise, I taught myself data science, diving into topics like machine learning, data analysis, and data visualization through hands-on projects and real-world applications.

Today, I combine my deep foundation in mathematics with data science to extract insights, build predictive models, and solve complex problems. I'm always eager to learn, explore new tools, and take on challenging projects that create meaningful impact.
